Thit Kho Trung

Description

This dish is extremely familiar to all Vietnamese households. It's the dish that Vietnamese moms would make and have their children eat for 2 weeks. It's a comforting food that reminds all Vietnamese people of their childhood. Thit Kho Trung is filled with beautiful flavors yet it is such a simple dish to make.

This savory,salty, and slightly sweet recipe will definitely warm your heart!

Ingredients

  • Pork Belly/Pork Shoulder
  • Water
  • Rico Coconut Soda
  • Fish Sauce
  • Salt
  • Nuoc Mau (Caramel Coloring)
  • Hard Boiled Eggs
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Cut your meat and parboil them to remove impurities. After this, put your clean meat into a pot.
  2. Add your coconut soda, fish sauce, and salt into a pat. Then add water into the pot until it covers the pork.
  3. Turn your heat to high until it starts boiling then turn it down to let it simmer. Make sure to:
    • Cover for the first 40-ish minutes
    • Check on your pot and stir every 20 minutes
  4. Make your nuoc mau and put it into the pot and add your eggs and onions within the last 40 minutes of cooking your Thit Kho Trung.
  5. Plate and serve. Enjoy!(:
