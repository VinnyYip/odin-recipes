Taiwanese Beef Noodle Soup

Description

Taiwanese Beef Noodle Soup is one of the most tasty dishes from Taiwan. It consists of a rich and spicy broth, tender beef, noodles, bok choy, and garnished with mustard greens, scallions, and cilantro.

Although the flavors of the soup is extremely complex and beautiful, it is really simple to make. This makes the dish all the more better for being able to cook this beautiful dish at home.

Ingredients

  • Beef Shank
  • Onion
  • Scallion
  • Ginger
  • Dried Red Chillies
  • Doubanjian Chili Paste
  • Fresh Noodles
  • Salt
  • Tomatoes
  • Shaoxing Wine
  • Soy Sauce
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Bay Leaf
  • Cinnamon Stick
  • Star Anise
  • Beef Broth
  • Brown Sugar

Steps

  1. Prepare your vegetables and cut them (Onion, Scallion, Ginger. Tomatoes)
  2. Cook your meats on high heat to get a quick sear then remove your meats
  3. Put all your prepared ingredients(except the vegetables) into the same pan and cook them. Once they're cooked, put your tomatoes in and add brown sugar, doubanjian, shaoxing wine, light soy sauce, and dark soy sauce. Mix. Once mixed, add your meats back in and mix around some more so the ingredients can be infused with them.
  4. Add everything that is in your pan/wok into a large pot. After that is done, add beef broth then bring to a boil. Once boiled, turn it down to low and let it simmer for 2 hours
  5. Prepare your noodles and vegetables into a different pot. Then plate up. Enjoy!(:
