Laab

Description

For anyone that is unfamiliar with Laotian food, this dish is an untouched flavor that your taste buds will fall in love with. Laab is savory, salty, and zingy.

This is one of the most easiest dishes to make just because you're combining all the ingredients into one bowl and mixing it.

Ingredients

  • Cilantro
  • Fish Sauce
  • Green Onion
  • Ground Dried Chili Pepper
  • Ground Pork
  • Limes
  • Shallots
  • Spearmint
  • Toasted Rice

Steps

  1. Squeeze lime onto the pork and mix then let it marinate for a few minutes
  2. In high heat add a bit of water and then your pork right after.
  3. Once your pork is cooked, add it into a large mixing bowl add your fish sauce, green onion, shallot, lime juice, toasted rice, and ground dried chili pepper. Mix and then serve with spearmint and toasted rice as garnish.
  4. Enjoy!(:
